id: unique indentifier
numero_dt: cadastral number according to the second “terrier” of the Sulpician Seminary
proprietai: name of the owner
acquisitio: start date of ownership
mode_acqui: mode of acquisition
dispositio : end date of ownership
mode_dispo: mode of disposition
origine : ethnic origin (F = French; C = Canadian)
occupation: occupational category
residence: place of residence (not always available)
sexe: M or F
type_propr: type of proprietor (individual, organization or estate of a deceased)
HISCO: occupational classification according to the HISCO standard
DBC: Dictionary of Canadian Biography link when applicable
url-ind-id: url to unique id
ind-id : individual id for people and organisations
